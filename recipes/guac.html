Guacamole

Description

Guacamole is a creamy, flavorful Mexican dip made primarily from ripe avocados. It's typically seasoned with lime juice, cilantro, onions, and sometimes tomatoes, creating a rich and tangy flavor.

Ingredients

  • Ripe avocados
  • Lime juice
  • Red onions (finely chopped
  • Fresh cilantro (chopped)
  • Tomato (diced, optional)
  • Salt
  • Black pepper
  • Garlic powder (optional)

Instructions

  1. Cut the avocados in half, remove the pit, and scoop the flesh into a mixing bowl.
  2. Mash the avocado with a fork or potato masher until it reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Add lime juice to the mashed avocado to prevent browning and enhance flavor.
