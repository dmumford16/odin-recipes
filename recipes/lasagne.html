Lasagne recipe

Recipe

Lasagna is a classic Italian dish made by layering wide, flat pasta sheets with rich, flavorful sauces like tomato, meat, and cheese. It's baked until the pasta is tender and the cheese on top is melted and golden, creating a hearty and comforting meal.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles
  • Ground beef or turkey
  • Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Shredded mozzarella cheese
  • Grated Parmesan cheese
  • Minced garlic
  • Chopped onions
  • Italian seasoning
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Olive oil

Instructions

  1. Boil the lasagna noodles according to package instructions until al dente.
  2. In a skillet over medium heat, sauté minced garlic and chopped onions in olive oil until translucent.
  3. Add ground beef or turkey to the skillet, breaking it up and cooking until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  4. Stir in the tomato sauce, Italian seasoning, salt, and pepper. Simmer the mixture for 10-15 minutes.
  5. In a separate bowl, combine ricotta cheese, half of the shredded mozzarella, and half of the grated Parmesan cheese.
  6. Preheat the oven to 375°F (190°C).
  7. Assemble the lasagna in layers in a baking dish: start with a thin layer of the meat sauce, followed by a layer of noodles, then a layer of the cheese mixture. Repeat until all ingredients are used, finishing with a layer of meat sauce on top.
  8. Sprinkle the remaining shredded mozzarella and grated Parmesan cheese over the top layer.
  9. Cover the baking dish with foil and bake for 25-30 minutes.
  10. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 10-15 minutes until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  11. Let the lasagna sit for a few minutes before slicing and serving. Enjoy your homemade lasagna!
